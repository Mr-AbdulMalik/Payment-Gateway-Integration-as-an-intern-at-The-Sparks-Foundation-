About Us

We are envisaging India where no one goes to sleep hungry. The project of this scale can not be implemented without active support of society. Support to Hunger Free India is true service of mankind. For those who have a little extra, it feels good to give back some to those who are without.

To join the program you can register yourself with us. You can support either by joining our volunteers to collect surplus food or you can sponsor some food packets. Each food packet will contain 4 roties and subji. Cost of one packet will be around Rs. 20.00.
